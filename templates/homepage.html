A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gasses (carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide) that are generated by our daily actions, assets, and activities. This usually consists of a holistic analysis of factors such as type of energy, choice of transportation, diet, and household energy. According to a study by Riebeek Holli of NASA, this footprint has risen close to 33 percent in the last 25 years alone, 75 percent of which was carbon dioxide.

Often, it may be hard to quantify exactly how much we as individuals contribute to this statistic. Hence, this web application was created in order to develop an understanding about the problem and based on a set of given parameters, to provide insights on how the user can reduce their footprint.
